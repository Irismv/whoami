SWOT

Strengths

I think my biggest strength is my ability to work in groups. I like to work together with other people and to take the lead to make sure that everything works out. I also feel like i'm good at making sure everyone has the opportunity to express their opinion.

Weaknesses

One of my biggest areas for improvement is my planning skills. I tend to delay my work until the last moment. This results in stressful moments that could've been avoided.

Opportunities

I see the opportunity to broaden my knowledge within the IT field. I hope that expanding my knowledge will eventually help me make a decision about which part of the IT field appeals to me the most.

Threats

I think my motivation can be a threat. I can be super enthusiastic about a project, but if i notice that my colleagues don't share the same enthousiasm as me then I tend to just stop working and let the project fail. This is something I have to prevent from happening.

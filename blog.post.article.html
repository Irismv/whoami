Cybersecurity, why is it so important?

In the world we live in today, we use internet for almost everything. From shopping the latest items to social media and work. Because we use it so much, it's important to protect our information and devices from threats.
Cybersecurity means protecting our networks, data and devices against viruses or hackers. Protecting these things aren't easy. Common threats are phising, malware or data leaks. For businesses, a good and strong cybersecurity is important. If personal information about their customers leaks, it would damage the business's reputation and it might result in money loss. For us as individuals, a good cyber security is also very important. If we don't protect our information the right way, it could result in our bank details or passwords becoming public. Always try to make a strong password, keep your security software up-to-date and turn on two-factor authentication. Doing this lowers the risk of being a victim to cyber attacks. To summarize, make sure to have good cybersecurity to prevent the (sadly common) attacks.

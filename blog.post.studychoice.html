My study choice

On my home page I already explained why I enrolled at this IT study. Here i'm going to explain why I decided to stay at this study so far. So, what do I like about IT? I really like the programming part about this study. I've always had an interest in how things work and with programming you can see exactly why something is or isn't working. I also like the designing side of the study. In school I used to draw a lot and I really enjoyed that, so its very fun to have that designing aspect here as well. I think the best part about this study is the fact that you can basically do anything. The possibilities are endless and I really like that. If you have a problem then there are multiple ways to solve it. I also really like the fact that the study just keeps developing itself. There are constantly new things to learn, so everyone just keeps on learning ( even the people with years and years of experience). To summarize: I like the fact that the study has so many different aspects where nothing is impossible and you're always expanding your knowledge

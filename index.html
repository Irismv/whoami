IT choice

Why did I decide to go for the IT study at the HZ? IT at the HZ wasn't really my first choice. I always liked playing videogames and learning about computers, but I never really thought that my career would have anything to do with those things. My first choice was to go to the police academy and become a detective. Sadly that wasn't an option anymore because I had to get 2 surgeries in a short amount of time. So I had to start looking for something else to do. An IT study still wasn't an option for me. I decided that i'd really like to work in a hospital. I went to the view day at the HZ in Vlissingen to see if the HZ was the right school for me. When I applied for the view day I could pick another study to look there as well. I decided to look at the IT study, because (like i mentioned before) I like videogames. I instantly liked the IT study. I liked it so much that I didn't even go to the study I originally came for. And here we are, a few months later and I'm on my way to become a IT professional. If you'd like to read more about my motivation to stay at the IT study, please go to my blog!

Future

What is my future going to look like? To be honest, I don't know.. I can however tell you how i'd like my future to look like. After I finished the IT study I really want to either continue studying at the uni and do something with cybersecurity or start my own business. If i start my own business I want the focus of my company to be about helping people in any way I can. I don't need to get rich with my work (altough it would be a bonus) as long as my work has helped a few people whilst still enjoying my work, then i'm happy!

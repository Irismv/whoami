Frequently asked questions
How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

There are two ways to print a document from your laptop. The first way is to go to mynetpay Find the Hz school and log in. Click on the option 'mobility print' and follow the further instructions. The second way is a bit easier. Scan the QR-code in the printing room, click on 'mobility printing' and follow further instructions.

How can you scan a document and send it to your laptop at HZ?

Firstly, go to the printing room. When you've arrived at the printing room, swipe your student card. Open the printer and place your document against the arrow in the corner. Now close the printer and choose the subject for your scan. Press start and it should print the document. Please don't forget to log out when your finished.

How can you book a project space?

There's a very specific step-by-step plan on how to do this on the hz website. I'm going to lead you to the step-by-step plan. Firstly you're going to go to the myhz site. You need to log in to follow the rest of the instructions. If you have trouble signing in, you should ask a teacher for help. You should see a page with the 'Welcome back' text. Click on the 'Change my personal menu' button. Find the 'Selfservice Portal' and add it to your home menu. Save the changes and head back to the 'Welcome back' page. Click on the Selfservice Portal and press the 'reservation' button. You should be able to see a stickman figure with a red background with the text 'How do I make a reservation?'. Follow the instructions on that page and you should be able to reserve a room!

What are the instructions if you want to park your car at the HZ parking lot?

How can you park at the Hz parking lot? Firstly park at the parking lot Koudsteensedijk. Walk to the Hz building and do the things you came for. When you're ready to leave, make a stop at the receptiondesk at JRCZ. Explain to the receptionist that you parked there and ask for your exit ticket. Walk back to your beautiful vehicle and drive of to anywhere you want!

How can I buy something on the hz webshop?

You can buy something on the hz webshop by following the next steps:

  1. Click on this link
  2. Find the products you want to order and add them to your shopping card.
  3. Now navigate to the shopping card and finish your order
How can I find my courses?

You can find your courses by logging in on the Myhz site. After you're logged in, you simply click 'learn.hz' and then you should see your courses. From here you just click on the course you'd like to see and after that you're all set!

Where can I find my grades?

You can find your grades by (again) going to the 'my.hz' page. Select the 'learn.hz' and you now should see the 'Dashboard' text on your screen. If you do see this, simply select your profile on the top right corner of the website and you should see a tab that says 'grades'! If you dont see the 'Dasboard' text, try clicking on the blue little home button and you should be able to see the text!

What can I do with my 'Student card'?

The student card is useful for a lot of things! You need it to print documents from your laptop, you can borrow books from the libary and you can enter the JRCZ ( If you completed the short trainingtest). You can only print documents if you have enough balance on your card. You can put more money on the card by using the ATM of the Hz. You'll find the ATM on the right side of the receptiondesk.
